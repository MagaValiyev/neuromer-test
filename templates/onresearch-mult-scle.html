Multiple Sclerosis Research


The Multiple Sclerosis Research Team, the branch of the Neuromer Research Team, will bring together undergraduate, graduate and doctorate students to conduct scientific research on Multiple Sclerosis. The Multiple Sclerosis Research Team provides a broad student network in the Multiple Sclerosis field.


Research

As the Multiple Sclerosis Research Team, we intend to connect with experts in neuroimmunology and write research articles. With the guidance of our mentor, we aim to take part in review and original research articles.

Article Writing

We will learn the tricks of writing articles from experienced students and adapt it to article works together.

Article Meetings

The Multiple Sclerosis Research Team will follow current developments in the field of Multiple Sclerosis. We will read topical articles published in high-impact journals. We will develop new hypotheses in our meetings where scientific articles are reviewed.

Laboratory Experience

We want to be a part of clinical and laboratory studies with our consultant who offers a new perspective on disease pathology in the field of Multiple Sclerosis. Our aim is to apply for projects for TÜBİTAK, TUSEB or international programs by catching internship experience in the laboratory of our advisor.

Who can apply to the Multiple Sclerosis Research Team?

All medical students who are interested in Multiple Sclerosis, volunteer and hardworking in academic research can apply to the Multiple Sclerosis Research Team. After the evaluation process, you can be a part of our team.

Associate Professor Serkan Demir

Our mentor: Assoc. Prof. Serkan DEMİR Associate Professor Serkan Demir is the advisor of the Multiple Sclerosis Research Team. Assoc. Dr. Serkan Demir graduated from Gülhane Military Medical Academy, Faculty of Medicine in 2006. Between 2009-2013, he completed his specialty training in neurology at GATA Haydarpaşa Training and Research Hospital. In 2019, he received the title of associate professor at SBU Sultan 2. Abdulhamid Han Training and Research Hospital. Associate Professor Serkan Demir was invited to Martyr Prof. Dr. İlhan Varank Sancaktepe Training and Research Hospital to establish a training clinic and he is still working as the neurology clinic chief. He conducts clinical and experimental studies on Multiple Sclerosis (MS), neuroimmunology and cerebrovascular diseases. He was the editor of “MS Reference, MS Patient Guide books and applications - Child Neurological Examination book” and author of chapters in many neurological books. He is a member of the Board of Directors of the Turkish MS Association responsible for projects. For the first time in the literature, he discussed the effects of COVID-19 in cohort studies with MS patients in Turkey. At the same time, targeting different therapeutic agents on MS and studies aiming to increase the quality of life of patients with MS are among his original researches. In addition, it participated in the European Academy of Neurology Congress 2023, held in Budapest this year, with 4 original researches. His highly cited articles published in national and international journals, his partnerships with international researchers, and the contribution of our advisor to the field of MS once again reveal.
