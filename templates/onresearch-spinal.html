Spinal Neurosurgery Research Team


Our mission is to unite passionate medical students with a shared interest in spinal neurosurgery, fostering an environment where collaborative learning expands our comprehension of spinal health.


Research

Within the Spinal Neurosurgery Research Team, our focus is to conduct insightful research and craft research articles, hand in hand with experts in the field of spinal neurosurgery.

Article Writing

We will engage in the art of article composition through mentorship from seasoned peers, honing our ability to communicate complex concepts with clarity.

Article Meetings

As a cohesive unit, we'll delve into the forefront of spinal neurosurgery developments by collectively studying scholarly articles. Guided by experienced members, we'll decode the nuances of scientific literature.

Presentation Meetings

Elevating our understanding of spinal neurosurgery, we will host presentation meetings, advancing not only our knowledge but also our presentation and communication skills.

Who can apply to the Spinal Neurosurgery Research Team?

Enthusiastic and diligent medical students intrigued by the intricacies of spinal neurosurgery, who possess a passion for collaborative learning, are welcome to join the Spinal Neurosurgery Research Team.


Assoc. Prof. Dr. Murat Sakir Eksi

Our mentor: Assist. Prof. Murat Sakir Eksi Assoc. Prof. Dr. Murat Sakir Eksi, graduated from Marmara University, Faculty of Medicine in 2007. Between 2007-2013, he completed his specialty training in neurosurgery at Marmara University under supervision of Prof. Dr. Necmettin Pamir, Prof. Dr. Mehmet Ozek, Prof. Dr. Turker Kilic. He was Post-doctoral Research Fellow in Spine Surgery 2013-2015 at UCSF. In 2019, he received the title of Associate Professor at Acibadem University. Murat Sakir EKSI is ECFMG certified, member of International Meningioma Society, member of The International Society for the Study of the Lumbar Spine. He is reviewer of journals such as Clinical Neurology and Neurosurgery, Journal of Spinal Cord Medicine, World Neurosurgery, Neurosurgical Review etc.. He is associate editor in Journal of Turkish Neurosurgery. Murat Sakir Eksi is the top-rated expert in Intervertebral Disc Generation, top-rated expert in Lumbar Vertebrae in Turkey during the years 2013-2023, in top 0.47% in research of Paraspinal Muscles worldwide.
